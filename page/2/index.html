Participate in a pursuit a whole life in length! If you ever wanted to get a totally different experience of a lifetime, BitLife is going to present such a chance to you. In this game you will be born once again, ageing, accomplishing certain aims, achieving the main purpose of your new living.

Gameplay BitLife

Start your adventure as a newborn who has a set of certain initial characteristics, such as the parents and their professions, the country where you are living and your current age. The more you develop, the more functions you can discover, so hurry up and explore the game from each side.

  • Get hold of random scenarios. Every round in BitLife is special, as you start your journey in an absolutely new role. You never know which set of peculiarities you are going to get next time, so the game guarantees an element of suspense.
  • Build strong relationships. No person can live without social interactions and BitLife game embraces every kind of them. Look for best friends during schooltime and university, search for your significant other and just exist in society.
  • Earn a fortune. As in reality, in this game money means a lot, so you have to make sure you have enough cash for all your ideas. Get a good job or find a faster way to become rich, BitLife suggests hundreds of options and you are free to pave your way as you wish.
  • Fulfil the greatest dreams. At the beginning of the game, the purpose of your life remains unknown, but once you start receiving certain skills and knowledge, you can formulate the main objective of this round, achieving it step by step.

All you need to do in BitLife game to progress is to make definite choices, according to your plans and intentions. Whether you wish to make your character a successful businessman or you want to generate an ordinary, but light-hearted involvement and have a big and cheerful family, you are able to pick any path you like.

Once you are done with the choices on a certain period of the game, you can skip time until your next birthday and see how your decisions altered your fate. Even the most insignificant details in BitLife can sometimes turn into important parts of your future narrative, so be careful with your actions and words!

Create New Opportunities

The only way to unlock additional options in this game is to craft them yourself. Check out the folders underneath and manage your living in multiple ways. Find a new job and earn more cash, look for a nice partner, get carried away with a hobby – unleash your creativity and try out everything BitLife offers!

At the same time, In BitLife you need to carry about the most basic things as well. There is a couple of bars that determine your current state and each of them needs to be fulfilled in one way or another:

  • Happiness
  • Health
  • Smarts
  • Looks

All your actions influence the game, so try not to leave this world too early, neglecting any of these crucial elements. Your choices in BitLife are carefully lined up in a true biography and in the end, you will have enough time to evaluate your achievements.
